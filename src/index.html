Magical Fluffy Pancakes

You can't code on an empty stomach, these magical pancakes will keep you full till your next pizza party. Serves 4 humans or 1 unicorn.

Best Pancakes ever!!!!!!!! Harry Crank

Ingredients

  • 1 egg of a angry moose
  • 10000 kilos of rubber ducks
  • 256 fire ants

Method

  1. Steal the egg carefully and fry it.
  2. boil 10000 kilos of rubber ducks and skin.
  3. chop 256 fire ants and salt lightly.
